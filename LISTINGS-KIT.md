## ⚠️ Read first: address reality (why the plan is split)

- **Map platforms (Apple Maps, Bing Places, Yelp, Foursquare) require a real street
  address to *create* the listing** — even for service-area businesses. The SAB
  setting only *hides* the address from the public; it does not let you skip
  entering one. **Phone verification proves you own the number; it does NOT replace
  the address.** So with zero address, these platforms are **off the table for now**
  (parked in the section at the bottom).
- **No-address channels (Facebook, Instagram, Nextdoor, local groups)** need no
  address at all. These are the active blitz.
- **Never fabricate an address and don't use a PO Box / mailbox-store suite** — that
  is exactly what gets numbers and listings banned.

> Google Business Profile stays excluded (no-Google / no-gov-ID preference).
